Passage 2

A NIGHT AT THE THEATRE






Susan and I are good friends. Yesterday we were at the local theater to see a new Broadway stage version of “Dracula.” The play was about a vampire in New York City, who fed on the blood of rich people. Susan and I are fans of vampire novels.

The play was quite scary and interesting. The costumes of the actors were very spectacular. The stage lighting and special effects were incredible. There were almost fifty actors in the play. We were really excited when we got an autograph from the leading actor. It was a lot of fun.
