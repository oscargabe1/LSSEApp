Passage 4

IN SEARCH OF A CAREER






Allan is going to enroll in the university of New York. He was going to study engineering but he decided that the Arts is the best choice for him. Since he is the president of his senior class, he is going to give a speech on graduation day. They are going to have a ball and a local popular band is going to play that night.

All his classmates are going to continue their studies at the university. They are looking forward to a bright future and an exciting career. Some of them are going to study business; others are going to study medicine. Allan is going to Rome in a couple of weeks for an Art History course. The art history course is going to be about the architectural infrastructure of Rome. Allan is learning Italian and practicing his pronunciation. He is going to visit his friends and family because he is going to be out of town for quite a while.
