Passage 4

IN SEARCH OF A CAREER






I am sitting at home doing my English homework. I am learning new sentences by repeating them aloud. I am memorizing the irregular verbs. I am practicing the verb excercises. I hope to be able to understand anyone having a conversation in English. My friends are studying at home, too.

Some of my fellow classmates are not doing their English homework assignment. They are not learning any new sentences or memorizing the irregular verbs. I know some of them are listening to the radio or watching television. They are eating or drinking. Some students are talking on the phone. What is Helen doing? Is she playing tennis? What am i doing? I am thinking of Helen. What are you thinking about? Are you eating?.
