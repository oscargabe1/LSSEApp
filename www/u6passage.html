Passage 6

GOING PLACES






John will soon be visiting his friend Hillary. Hillary lives in New York City. New York City is a huge city for somebody that lived all his life in a small city like Austin, Texas. John plans to visit Hillary this weekend, but he worries about getting lost in the Big Apple. So, he already bought a map of the city to get an idea of how big it is.

The main attractions he wants to visit are the Statue of Liberty, the Twin Towers, the David Letterman Show, that is located in the center of the city and of course visit his friend Hillary, who lives in Manhatta. John would also like to see some plays on Broadway. Hillary already plans on taking him to the best restaurants in the city. John loves to eat out so he expects to have a great experience.
