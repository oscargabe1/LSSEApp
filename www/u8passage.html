Passage 8

OFFICE DUTIES






Tom works in an accounting firm. Hanna is his personal assistant. Tom studied to become an accountant; he is in charge of the major accounts of the firm, and of attending clients. Hanna is in charge of answering the telephone, taking messages, setting appointments with Tom’s clients, arranging staff meetings, taking minutes of such meetings and basically handling Tom’s personal agenda. Tom’s daily office duties are so many that without Hanna’s help he would be in a lot of trouble just by managing his daily activities.

Among Tom’s duties are to assist clients in any request or doubt that they have concerning their accounts. Tom must know everything about their accounts and he needs to be up to date; so everyday he checks any changes or payments due in order for them to be corrected. If any mistakes were done on Tom’s behalf, he would not be happy because most of the mistakes in an accounting firm involve great amounts of money; in which the accountant is responsible for its proper handling. Also he is the one who takes care of paying dues on time; so that they won’t have any surcharges later on. Tom is really glad to have an assistant like Hanna; they make a great team working together.
