Expressions

91. TO BE BEIDE ONESELF

Estar fuera de sí, descontrolarse.

He was beside himself after the funeral.

92. TO STAY IN BED

Guardar reposo, quedarse en cama.

The doctor told him to stay in bed.

93. TO IGNORE

Hacer caso omiso, ignorar.

The runner ignored the instructor's directions.

94. TO EMPHASIZE

Destacar, hacer énfasis en.

The teacher emphasized the need to know the personal pronouns by heart.

95. TO FACE

Afrontar, enfrentarse con.

You have to face your problems.

96. HALF AND HALF

Mitad y mitad.

Let's go half and half on the price of the pizza.

97. (TO) PLAY FAIR

Ser justo; seguir las reglas; jugar limpio.

We play fair in business.

98. THE DAILY GRIND

La rutina diaria.

Everyone must face the daily grind.

99. THE EARLIEST POSSIBLE

Lo antes posible.

I need your answer at the earliest possible moment.

100. BETTER LATE THAN NEVER

Más vale tarde que nunca.

The doctor arrived late, but better late than never.
