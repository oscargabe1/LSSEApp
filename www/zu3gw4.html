Expressions

21. (TO) GET THE RUN AROUND

Traer de aquí para allá.

I was getting the run around at the bank.

22. TO BE ASHAMED

Avergonzarse.

Billy is ashamed of not passing math.

23. COME DOWN WITH

Darle a uno (enfermedad).

He is coming down with a cold.

24. OUT OF THE BLUE

De la nada.

He came up with the right answer out of the blue.

25. SORE THROAT

Dolor de garganta.

Diane has a sore throat.

26. NOWADAYS

Hoy en día, actualmente.

Nowadays, computers facilitate everything.

27. THOSE WHO

Aquellos que.

Those who know the answer raise your hand.

28. (TO) DROP (CHANGE) THE SUBJECT

Cambiar de tema.

He tried to drop the subject immediately.

29. KICK UP A STORM

Armar un escándalo.

Paul's little brother was kicking up a storm.

30. ANGRY ABOUT (SOMETHING)

Enojarse (por algo).

What are you angry about?
