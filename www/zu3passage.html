Passage 3

ALL THE LAND
THAT A MAN NEEDS







Many years ago, lived a farmer who wanted to be rich and own many acres of land. A stranger told him of some land for sale, nearby. The farmer bought the land. He was content for a while but then again, he wished to own even more land. The stranger told him of some additional land for sale. The farmer bought it again. This happened several times. The farmer bought more and more land but still was not satisfied.

One day, the stranger said that he had a friend some distance away. This friend could sell the farmer all the land he needed for only $300 dollars. The farmer went to visit the friend, whom he noticed, looked a lot like the stranger himself. The man said that for $300 dollars the farmer could have all the land he wanted. He could buy all the land he could walk on from sunrise to sunset. The farmer was supposed to walk as far as possible, mark the spot, and return before sunset. If he arrived late, he would lose both the land and the money.

The farmer agreed and went to bed that night in order to be well rested for his trip the next day. He did not sleep well. He dreamed that it was the devil himself who was tempting him. His greed for land was so great, however, that at sunrise he was dressed and ready to leave. He walked and ran all morning in order to cover as much ground as possible. On the return trip he was very tired. He walked slowly, and at the end he had to run a great distance in order to get back before sunset. When he arrived, he fell down exhausted. He died without ever getting up.

At the burial a few days later, the stranger had a smile on his face. The stranger remarked: “Now the old man has all the land he needs, exactly six feet in which to lie. I hope he’s satisfied.”
