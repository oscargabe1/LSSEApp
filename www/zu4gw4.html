Expressions

31. BY NOW

A esta hora, ya.

By now, they must be arriving in Italy.

32. TO STAND IN LINE

Hacer fila, hacer cola.

Westood in line for more than two hours.

33. (EVER) SINCE THEN

Desde entonces.

Ever since then she became very sensible.

34. WHOOPS!

¡Huy!

Whoops! I almost fell.

35. SIT UP STRAIGHT

Siéntese derecho, siéntese bien.

Would you please sit up straight?

36. RAISE YOUR HAND

Levante su mano.

Raise your hand if you know the answer.

37. THERE IS NO TIME TO LOSE

No hay tiempo que perder.

We must hurry; there is no time to lose.

38. THE CLASS IS OVER

La case ha terminado.

Students, the class is over.

39. GO AWAY

Váyase, lárguese.

Go away, leave me alone!

40. STOP SHOUTING

Deje de gritar.

Stop shouting! I don't want to hear you.
