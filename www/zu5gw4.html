Expressions

41. AT LEAST

Por lo menos.

Give me at least one reason why you don't want to go.

42. TO TAKE A RIDE

Pasear, dar un paseo (en carro).

We took a ride in the afternoon.

43. TO RUN AFTER

Correr detrás de.

Bill is running after John, they are playing football.

44. OH BOY!

¡Qué padre!.

Oh boy! You are going with us.

45. OUT OF TOWN

Fuera de la ciudad.

My boss is out of town at a convention.

46. ON THE OTHER HAND

Por otra parte.

On the other hand, I prefer to go alone to my house.

47. TO HAVE A GOOD TIME

Divertirse, pasarsela bien.

I hope you have a good time at the party.

48. TO KEEP IN MIND

Tener en mente, tomar en cuenta, recordar.

The project is going well, keep in mind that we started it two weeks late.

49. TO HAND IN

Entregar (a mano).

Hand in your test papers.

50. TO FALL IN LOVE WITH

Enamorarse de.

Do you fall in love with a girl's smile?
