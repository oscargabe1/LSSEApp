Expressions

61. AS WELL AS

Así como; tan bien como.

I like spaghetti as well as pizza.

62. FOR HEAVEN'S SAKE!

¡Por el amor de Dios!.

Don't do that for heaven's sake!

63. NO KIDDING

De veras, en serio; no estar bromeando.

No kidding, Paul is going to the party.

64. UP TO NOW

Hasta ahora.

Up to now, you are the most outstanding student in school.

65. INSTEAD OF

En vez de, en lugar de.

Instead of watching television you should be studying.

66. TO PASS AWAY

Morirse, fallecer.

Her grandfather passed away.

67. TO CATCH FLAT-FOOTED

Tomar de sorpresa.

Your arrival caught me flat-footed.

68. TO TAKE AFTER

Parecerse a, salir a.

She takes after her mother's side.

69. TO BE ALL EARS

Ser todo oídos.

Iam all ears when someone is talking about an interesting subject.

70. I AM HAPPY TO SEE (YOU)

Me da gusto ver(te).

I am happy to see you, Peter.
