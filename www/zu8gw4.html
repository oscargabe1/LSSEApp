Expressions

71. TO BE THIRSTY

Tener sed.

When I am thirsty, I like to drink cold tea.

72. TO BE HUNGRY

Tener hambre.

My friend is hungry.

73. SO LONG

Hasta luego.

So long, we will see you tomorrow.

74. IN THE OPEN AIR

Al aire libre; de conocimiento público.

The concert was held in the open air.

75. OTHERWISE

De otro modo, de otra manera.

It cannot be otherwise.

76. (TO) REPLY AT ONCE

Contestar inmediatamente.

The letter read: "Reply at once".

77. TO PAY TRIBUTE TO

Rendir homenaje a.

The students paid tribute to their teachers.

78. TO DO ONE'S BEST

Hacer lo mejor (de uno).

I do my best to please you.

79. (TO HAVE A LUMP IN MY THROAT)

Tener un nudo en la garganta.

When Susan told me about her mother's death Ihad a lump in my throat.

80. (TO) LIKE THIS BETTER

Preferir este, este me gusta más.

I like this better than that one.
